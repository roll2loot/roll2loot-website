Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Roll2Loot Studio LLC operates the NPC Generator mobile application (“App”).

Information Collection

The NPC Generator app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app:

  • Does not use advertising services

All generated NPCs and saved favorites are stored locally on the user's device only.

The app is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect data in accordance with its own privacy policies. Roll2Loot Studio LLC does not have access to or control over that data.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data Deletion

Because no data is collected or stored externally, deleting the app removes all locally stored data from the device.
